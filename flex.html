MY GRANDMOTHER

I wanted to introduce to you my wonderful and loving grandmother. She's been a constant source of love, support, and encouragement in my life. She's always there to lend a listening ear, offer wise advice, or simply share a warm smile. My grandmother supported me by always there for me and taking care of me since I was a kid. She made me the luckiest boy in the world. When I was a kid she's always there to fix my wounds, and until now when I was in adulthood she's still there for me when I'm feeling sad and down. She's always there for me through ups and downs. She's my hero, my inspiration, and my happy pill. I'm so fortunate to have her in my life. I hope you'll have the chance to get to know her better.
